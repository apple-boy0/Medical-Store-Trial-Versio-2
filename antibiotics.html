Antibiotics

Amoxicillin

How it works: Kills bacteria by inhibiting cell wall synthesis.

Lethal Dosage: Overdose can cause severe diarrhea, allergic reactions.

Allergic Reaction: Rash, itching, swelling.

Suitable Age: Adults and children; dosage varies.

Azithromycin

How it works: Stops bacterial protein synthesis to kill bacteria.

Lethal Dosage: Overdose may cause heart rhythm problems.

Allergic Reaction: Rash, dizziness.

Suitable Age: Adults and children; consult doctor for dosage.

Ciprofloxacin

How it works: Kills bacteria by interfering with DNA replication.

Lethal Dosage: Overdose may cause seizures and tendon damage.

Allergic Reaction: Rash, swelling, joint pain.

Suitable Age: Adults; avoid in young children.
