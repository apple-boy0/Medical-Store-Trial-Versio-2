Painkillers

Paracetamol

How it works: Reduces fever and relieves mild to moderate pain.

Lethal Dosage: Overdose can cause liver damage.

Allergic Reaction: Rare allergic rash or swelling.

Suitable Age: Adults and children above 2 years; avoid in infants without advice.

Ibuprofen

How it works: Non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID), reduces pain, fever, and inflammation.

Lethal Dosage: High doses may cause stomach bleeding or kidney damage.

Allergic Reaction: Rash, itching, swelling, dizziness.

Suitable Age: Adults and children above 6 months.

Aspirin

How it works: NSAID, reduces pain, fever, inflammation and thins blood.

Lethal Dosage: Excessive doses can cause stomach bleeding and toxicity.

Allergic Reaction: Rash, swelling, asthma attacks.

Suitable Age: Adults; avoid in children with viral infections.
