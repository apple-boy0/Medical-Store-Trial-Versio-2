General (Travel) Medicines

ORS Sachet

How it works: Rehydrates the body by replenishing fluids and electrolytes.

Lethal Dosage: Generally safe; excess intake may cause imbalance.

Allergic Reaction: Rare.

Suitable Age: All ages but consult doctor for babies.

Antacid Tablets

How it works: Neutralizes stomach acid to relieve heartburn.

Lethal Dosage: Excess may cause constipation or diarrhea.

Allergic Reaction: Rare skin reactions.

Suitable Age: Adults and children over 5 years.

Motion Sickness Tablets

How it works: Reduces nausea and dizziness during travel.

Lethal Dosage: Overdose may cause drowsiness or confusion.

Allergic Reaction: Rare.

Suitable Age: Adults and children over 2 years.
